Our Story

Built on a Vision of
Agribusiness Excellence

MindKraft Farm Venture was founded with a clear conviction: that Osun State deserves a professional, reliable, and ethically managed poultry supply chain. Nestled in Osogbo — the capital of Osun State — our farm sits at the heart of one of Nigeria's most vibrant regional food economies, serving a growing population with rising demand for quality protein.

We specialise in premium Arbor Acres and Cobb 500 broiler breeds, raised over a disciplined 6-week cycle and harvested at an ideal weight of 2.2 kg per bird. Our production model is designed for reliability — running multiple cycles per year means our customers can count on fresh birds arriving at regular, predictable intervals, eliminating the supply gaps that plague informal poultry chains.

With a dedicated on-site borehole, professional brooding infrastructure, a fully equipped chicken house, and a team of trained farm workers supervised by an experienced Farm Manager, MindKraft is built to scale. Every decision — from sourcing day-old chicks to final-mile delivery — is made with quality, food safety, and your satisfaction as the guiding standard.

How We Operate

From Day-Old Chick
to Your Table

Every bird at MindKraft Farm passes through a precise, supervised 6-week production cycle. Strict biosecurity, optimised nutrition, and daily health monitoring ensure that only the highest-quality broilers reach our buyers. Here is exactly how we do it.

1
Procurement
Certified day-old chicks (DOCs) are sourced from licensed, disease-free commercial hatcheries. Each batch is inspected on arrival for health and uniformity before entering the facility.
2
Brooding Phase
Weeks 1–2: Chicks are placed in a temperature-regulated brooding area (32–35°C, reducing weekly). Electric brooders and drinkers ensure warmth, hydration, and early nutritional uptake — critical for uniform growth.
3
Growth & Health
Weeks 3–6: Birds receive graded feed (starter → grower → finisher). Full vaccine protocols — Newcastle Disease, Gumboro, and IBD — are administered by schedule. Daily welfare checks are logged.
4
Harvest & Dispatch
At week 6, birds averaging 2.2 kg are harvested. Live birds are available for collection; dressed orders are processed same day. Dispatch is coordinated with buyers to ensure freshness on arrival.

Standards & Quality Assurance

Committed to
Food Safety
at Every Stage

At MindKraft, food safety is not a compliance checkbox — it is a core operating principle. From the day chicks arrive to the moment birds are dispatched, every step is governed by documented protocols designed to ensure the birds reaching your household or business are wholesome, safe, and free from preventable disease.

Our veterinary programme is managed in partnership with a qualified animal health practitioner who oversees all disease prevention and treatment decisions. No antibiotics are administered without professional direction, and no bird is harvested within the withdrawal period of any drug.

Full Vaccination Programme
Newcastle Disease Virus (NDV), Infectious Bursal Disease (Gumboro), and Infectious Bronchitis vaccines are administered to schedule every cycle. Records are maintained per batch.
Independent Borehole Water Supply
Our on-site borehole provides clean, uncontaminated water through a dedicated 500-litre overhead tank. Birds never rely on public water supply — maintaining consistent hydration and reducing the risk of waterborne pathogens.
Graded Feed Programme
Birds receive appropriately phased feeds — starter (weeks 1–2), grower (weeks 3–4), and finisher (weeks 5–6) — totalling 104 bags per cycle from reputable manufacturers. Feed conversion efficiency is monitored weekly.
Professional, Trained Farm Team
Our Head of Operations, Farm Manager, and Labour Worker follow written Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs) for every aspect of farm management — from litter management and biosecurity to daily feed and mortality logging.
